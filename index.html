Essential Workout Tips

Stay Hydrated

Drink water before, during, and after your workout. Aim for 16-20 oz before exercise and 8-10 oz every 15 minutes during activity.

Proper Warm-Up

Always start with 5-10 minutes of light cardio and dynamic stretching to prevent injury and improve performance.

Nutrition Timing

Eat a light meal 2-3 hours before workout and have a protein-rich snack within 30 minutes after exercise.

Rest & Recovery

Allow adequate rest between workouts and aim for 7-9 hours of sleep per night for optimal recovery.

Progressive Overload

Gradually increase intensity, weight, or duration to continue seeing improvements in fitness levels.

Listen to Your Body

Pay attention to pain vs. discomfort and adjust your workout intensity accordingly.
