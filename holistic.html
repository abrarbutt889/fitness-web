FAQs

What is holistic fitness?

Holistic fitness is an approach that considers the whole person - body, mind, and spirit - in the quest for optimal health and wellness.

How do I start a holistic practice?

Begin with simple daily practices like mindful breathing, gentle movement, and connecting with nature. Gradually integrate more elements as you progress.

Can I combine holistic practices with traditional exercise?

Yes! Holistic practices complement traditional exercise by adding mindfulness, breathing awareness, and energy work to physical movement.
