FAQs

How often should I train each muscle group?

Generally, each major muscle group should be trained 2-3 times per week with adequate rest between sessions.

What's the best way to prevent injury?

Focus on proper form, warm up adequately, and progress gradually with weights. Listen to your body and don't ego lift.

How important is nutrition for strength training?

Nutrition is crucial for muscle growth and recovery. Ensure adequate protein intake and maintain a balanced diet.
